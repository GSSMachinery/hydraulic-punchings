About Us

Gulf States Saw & Machine Co., Inc. has been providing professional fabrication equipment to professional fabricators since 1992. Based out of Hueytown, Alabama, our headquarters is centrally located within our ten state territory. Gulf States Saw & Machine Co., Inc. is the sister company to Alabama Tool & Supply Co., Inc. which was founded in 1979. We are a family owned and operated business.

Gulf States Saw & Machine Co., Inc. operates the largest saw blade shop in the southeast and has more than 50,000 products available to our customers through our supply catalog. Tending to our customers needs throughout the year is just as important as being there for the large machinery purchases.
